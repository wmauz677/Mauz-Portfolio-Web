Work Experience

Callaway Golf

During the summer of 2018 I worked as an operations intern with Callaway Golf Company. The job was located in Carlsbad, CA and the company headquarters.

At this job, I worked with program managers to optimize the completion of various club programs. I also helped with the logistics of global golf ball distribution.

I was able to travel to the assembly facility and distribution center to gain a stronger understanding of the supply chain.

Traylor Brothers - Underground

I worked with Traylor Brothers in the Fall of 2017 as a heading engineer intern on the Regional Connector Project in Los Angeles. This is a $1.7 Billion Metro project that is adding new connections to the existing metro system in downtown L.A.

This is a joint venture between Traylor Brothers and Skanska called "Regional Connector Constructors." We used an EPB Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) to dig 1-mile twin tunnels along the route. Thre metro stations are currently being built along this new route.

I spent my shifts in the heading of the TBM performing quality control, ring senquencing, and routine maintenance of the machine.

Kiewit Mining Group

During the summer of 2015 I worked as a mechanical engineering intern with Kiewit Mining Group. The job was located in Soda Springs, Idaho, on an open pit phosphate mine.

The first month and a half of the internship was physical labor; working with the maintenance crew in order to get a better understanding of the impact of the engineer's job.

For the remainder of the internship, I coordinated mine activities with maintenance planners and managers. I used SAP and Excel to monitor and schedule maintenance, parts deliveries, and repairs on heavy machinery.
