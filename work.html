Work Experience

Kiewit Mining Group

During the summer of 2015 I worked as a mechanical engineering intern with Kiewit Mining Group. The job was located in Soda Springs, Idaho, on an open pit phosphate mine.

The first month and a half of the internship was physical labor; working with the maintenance crew in order to get a better understanding of the impact of the engineer's job.

For the remainder of the internship, I coordinated mine activities with maintenance planners and managers. I used SAP and Excel to monitor and schedule maintenance, parts deliveries, and repairs on heavy machinery.

Traylor Brothers - Underground

I am currently working with Traylor Brothers as a heading engineer intern on the Regional Connector Project in Los Angeles. This is a $1.7 Billion Metro project that is adding new connections to the existing metro system in downtown L.A.

This is a joint venture between Traylor Brothers and Skanska called "Regional Connector Constructors." We are using an EPB Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) to construct 1-mile twin tunnels along with building three new metro stations.

I spend my shift in the heading of the TBM performing quality control, ring senquencing, and routine maintenance of the machine.
