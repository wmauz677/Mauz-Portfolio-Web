Photography

A Few Words

I began taking photos as a way to capture memories and express my creativity. Over time, photography has led me to some of the most beautiful places I can imagine. As I continue to see such beauty in the environment, I am driven to help protect it. I plan to someday use my photography to help raise awareness about climate change and push to protect our natural landscapes. I believe that everyone has the power to make a difference, and this is one way that I believe that I can promote postive change.

I have never used my photography in a professional setting, though I am open to opportunities for projects that I believe in. I am always looking for like-minded individuals for larger-scale projects, as I believe that a team is always stronger than an individual. Please reach out if you would like to discuss potential opportunities.
