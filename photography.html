Photography

A few words

Photography has brought me to places that I never thought I would be able to see. Being able to capture the beauty of the environment reminds me every day how much I want to work to protect it. I hope that my photography can bring those same thoughts to more people, because everyone can work towards a lifestyle that is more conscientious of the planet.

I will someday use this photography to help the world become a better place. These photos, in conjunction with a website, plentiful information on environmentally sound practices, and community interaction, can help people protect the environment. In a world where waste is plentiful, change is important and necessary moving forward into the future.
